CHS Reservations

The CHS Reservations app is an essential tool for Corporate Hospitality Services (CHS) clients, designed to provide a seamless and efficient way to manage hospitality needs. The app offers the ability to:

  • Book new reservations quickly and efficiently.
  • View and modify existing reservations with ease.
  • Access and manage account and contact information.
  • Review invoices and accruals with transparent details.
  • Track reservation history and view confirmation details.

All data is securely stored within CHS internal systems, ensuring privacy and confidentiality. Designed exclusively for CHS clients, the app is tailored to meet the high standards of corporate hospitality management.

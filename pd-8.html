Transforming Document Chaos into Structured Workflows

Sharma Hub is a custom-built platform designed to handle the real-world complexity of tax season. We developed a system that organizes high-volume document intake, routes work across accounting teams, and provides full visibility into operations.

The platform operates continuously at scale, enabling teams to process thousands of client documents efficiently while maintaining accuracy and control.

The Challenge

Sharma Tax faced a high-volume operational challenge during tax season. Thousands of documents were arriving from multiple sources including email, uploads, and mobile devices. Files were inconsistent, workflows were fragmented, and visibility into processing status was limited.

The team needed a system that could:

  • Handle large-scale document intake
  • Organize unstructured data automatically
  • Support multiple team members working in parallel
  • Provide real-time visibility into workflow status
  • Improve client communication without increasing workload

Our Solution

We built a comprehensive document workflow platform featuring:

  • Automated document ingestion from multiple sources
  • AI-assisted categorization and organization of files (W2s, 1099s, etc.)
  • Ticketing system that converts documents into structured units of work
  • Workflow engine for assigning and tracking tasks across the team
  • Real-time client portal with status tracking (Domino's-style experience)
  • Smart notification system for internal and client updates
  • Operational dashboards providing real-time insights into workflow performance
  • Secure infrastructure with no model training on client data
  • Scalable architecture designed for high-volume seasonal demand

Results

The Sharma Hub platform successfully operated through peak tax season at scale.

  • 40,000+ documents processed
  • 2,000+ clients managed
  • 350,000+ workflow events tracked
  • 24/7 system uptime during peak demand
  • Minimal bugs and near-zero downtime

The platform enabled Sharma Tax to streamline operations, improve team efficiency, and provide a significantly better client experience.

Impact

Sharma Hub transformed a traditionally manual, fragmented process into a structured and scalable system. Teams were able to focus on processing returns instead of organizing files. Clients gained visibility into their status. Leadership gained insight into operations through real-time analytics.

The result was a more efficient, reliable, and scalable workflow that performed under real-world conditions.

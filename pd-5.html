Small apps with big ideas.

We have built a number of simple kids apps, some are silly games while others are educational. Many of these apps come with in-app purchases and subscription plans. We can deliver basic kids based educational and play apps within 3 to 4 weeks.

The Challenge

Startups needed cost-effective mobile apps that could be developed quickly without sacrificing quality. The apps needed to work seamlessly on both iOS and Android, include monetization features, and provide engaging experiences for young users.

Our Solution

We developed a streamlined mobile app development process featuring:

  • Cross-platform development using Ionic and React Native
  • Reusable component library for faster development
  • Integrated payment processing for in-app purchases
  • Subscription management with auto-renewal
  • Engaging animations and interactive elements
  • Parental controls and child-safe features
  • Analytics for user behavior and engagement tracking
  • App Store and Google Play optimization

Results

We successfully launched over 15 mobile apps with an average development time of 3-4 weeks. Apps achieved an average 4.5-star rating across app stores, with several reaching top 100 in their categories. The rapid development approach reduced costs by 60% compared to native development while maintaining high quality standards.
